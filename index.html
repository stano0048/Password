      How the checker works

      1. Rewards passwords with higher length.
      2. Checks lowercase, uppercase, digits, and symbols.
      3. Penalizes repeated characters and common weak patterns.
      4. Estimates entropy from the character pool used.
      5. Provides feedback that users can act on immediately.
